School Management System

This application provides on-time information, and shares specified information via any Android and iOS-operated mobile phone. Management, staff, students, teachers, and other persons involved in institutions can get access to and update any information instantly. The object is not only to gather information; it also gives a chance to monitor students to fulfill parents’ demands.

Admin Features

  • Manage Student & Staff Information (lists, details)
  • Oversee Leave Requests (approve/reject for students & staff)
  • Manage Academic Resources (attendance, study materials)
  • Configure Institutional Structures (fees, library, transport, dormitory & rooms)
  • Handle Library & Transport Logistics (add books/members, create routes, assign vehicles/drivers)
  • Facilitate Online Communication (schedule & manage Zoom meetings)

Teacher Features

  • Access Student Information & Academic Records
  • Manage Student Attendance (Set & View)
  • Oversee Homework Lifecycle (upload assignments & evaluate student submissions)
  • Share Academic Content (upload/delete study materials & view school notices)
  • Handle Personal & Resource Management (apply for leave & view library books)
  • Conduct Online Interactions (host Zoom classes/meetings)

Student Features

  • Access Academic Essentials (class routine, subjects, timeline)
  • Engage with Learning Materials (view/download study materials, view/submit homework)
  • Track Academic Progress (class attendance, examination schedules & results - online/offline)
  • Manage Personal Requests & Stay Informed (apply for leave, view school notices)
  • Access Campus Resources (library books/assigned, transport routes, dormitory details)
  • Participate in Online Learning & Communication (attend Zoom classes, contact teachers)

Parents Features

  • Monitor Children's Profiles (view children list & individual dashboards)
  • Track Academic Performance (homework status, exam results for each child)
  • Stay Updated on Child's School Life (attendance, leave status, fee assignments)
  • Receive Important School Communications (school notices, study material updates for child)
  • Engage with School Remotely (join parent-teacher Zoom meetings)
  • Receive Key Alerts via Push Notifications (for homework, exams, fees, etc.)

Push Notifications (Key Alerts For)

  • Homework Updates: Creation (to students/parents), submission (to teacher), evaluation (to student).
  • Academic Milestones: Exam creation (to students/parents), study material additions (to students).
  • Financial Matters: Fee assignment and approval (to students/parents).
  • Leave Management: Approval or rejection of leave requests (to students/teachers).
  • Attendance Records: Notification to students when attendance is marked.
